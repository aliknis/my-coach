Mediterranean Diet

A heart-healthy, balanced eating pattern centered on whole foods, olive oil, and lean proteins.

Plan Overview

  • Best for: General health and long-term adherence.
  • Macro target: Protein 25%, Carbs 45%, Fat 30%
  • Meal structure: 3 main meals with optional healthy snacks.
  • Main focus: Cardiovascular and metabolic health.

Recommended Foods

  • Olive oil, nuts, seeds, and legumes
  • Fish, poultry, yogurt, and moderate dairy
  • Whole grains, fruits, and vegetables
  • Herbs and spices instead of excess salt

Coaching Tips

  • Build meals around vegetables and whole grains.
  • Choose fish regularly for healthy fats.
  • Keep consistency with simple meal prep.
