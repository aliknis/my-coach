Calorie Surplus

A structured surplus strategy designed to support muscle gain, performance, and recovery.

Plan Overview

  • Best for: Lean bulking and strength phases.
  • Macro target: Protein 25%, Carbs 50%, Fat 25%
  • Meal structure: 4-6 meals with regular carb intake.
  • Main focus: Controlled weight and muscle gain.

Recommended Foods

  • Lean proteins: poultry, fish, eggs, dairy
  • Complex carbs: rice, oats, potatoes, whole grain pasta
  • Healthy calorie boosters: olive oil, nut butters, nuts
  • Fruits and vegetables for micronutrient coverage

Coaching Tips

  • Increase calories gradually to limit excess fat gain.
  • Track body weight and gym performance weekly.
  • Keep protein and sleep consistent for recovery.
