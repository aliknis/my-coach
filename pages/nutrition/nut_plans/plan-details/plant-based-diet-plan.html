Plant-Based Diet

A vegetarian/vegan-friendly framework focused on whole plant foods for health and sustainability.

Plan Overview

  • Best for: Ethical eating and high-fiber nutrition.
  • Macro target: Protein 25%, Carbs 55%, Fat 20%
  • Meal structure: 3-5 meals emphasizing legumes and whole grains.
  • Main focus: Nutrient-dense plant variety.

Recommended Foods

  • Beans, lentils, tofu, tempeh, edamame
  • Whole grains like oats, quinoa, and brown rice
  • Vegetables, fruits, nuts, and seeds
  • Fortified alternatives for B12 and calcium support

Coaching Tips

  • Combine different protein sources daily.
  • Prioritize iron, B12, and omega-3 intake.
  • Plan meals ahead to keep variety high.
