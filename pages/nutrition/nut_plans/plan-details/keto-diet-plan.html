Keto Diet

A high-fat, low-carb plan that supports ketosis for fat loss and stable energy.

Plan Overview

  • Best for: Fat loss and appetite control.
  • Macro target: Fat 70-75%, Protein 20-25%, Carbs 5-10%
  • Meal structure: 3-4 meals focused on healthy fats and protein.
  • Main focus: Entering and maintaining ketosis.

Recommended Foods

  • Eggs, fatty fish, chicken, beef
  • Avocado, olive oil, nuts and seeds
  • Low-carb vegetables like spinach and broccoli
  • Cheese and full-fat dairy (if tolerated)

Coaching Tips

  • Hydrate well and keep electrolytes balanced.
  • Track net carbs daily for consistency.
  • Use whole foods instead of processed keto snacks.
