High Protein Diet

A protein-forward nutrition strategy built for muscle growth, recovery, and body recomposition.

Plan Overview

  • Best for: Muscle gain and preserving lean mass.
  • Macro target: Protein 40%, Carbs 35%, Fat 25%
  • Meal structure: 4-6 meals with protein in each.
  • Main focus: Recovery and muscle protein synthesis.

Recommended Foods

  • Chicken breast, eggs, fish, lean beef
  • Greek yogurt, cottage cheese, protein shakes
  • Rice, oats, fruit, and potatoes for training energy
  • Vegetables and healthy fats for micronutrients

Coaching Tips

  • Distribute protein evenly across meals.
  • Pair carbs around workouts for performance.
  • Keep hydration high to support recovery.
