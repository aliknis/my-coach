Calorie Deficit

A balanced approach that reduces calorie intake to support steady, sustainable fat loss.

Plan Overview

  • Best for: Beginners and long-term weight loss.
  • Macro target: Protein 30%, Carbs 40%, Fat 30%
  • Meal structure: 3-5 balanced meals with portion control.
  • Main focus: Consistent weekly calorie deficit.

Recommended Foods

  • Lean proteins like chicken, fish, and tofu
  • Whole grains, potatoes, oats, and rice
  • Fruits and high-volume vegetables
  • Healthy fats from nuts, seeds, and olive oil

Coaching Tips

  • Aim for gradual weight loss week to week.
  • Prioritize protein and fiber for satiety.
  • Track intake consistently and adjust slowly.
