Full Body Split

Each workout trains all major muscle groups, making it simple and highly effective for beginners and busy schedules.

Program Overview

  • Best for: Beginners and people with 3-4 days available.
  • Frequency: 3-4 days/week
  • Session duration: 45-60 minutes
  • Main focus: Base strength, conditioning, and movement skill

Suggested Weekly Structure

  • Day 1: Full Body A
  • Day 2: Rest
  • Day 3: Full Body B
  • Day 4: Rest
  • Day 5: Full Body C (optional)

Recommended Core Exercises

  • Squat
  • Bench Press
  • Row
  • Romanian Deadlift
  • Lat Pulldown
  • Core work

Coaching Tips

  • Start with compound lifts first.
  • Use moderate volume and clean form.
  • Increase load gradually each week.
