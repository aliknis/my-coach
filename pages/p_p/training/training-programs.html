Push/Pull/Legs (PPL)

Split your week into three days: Push (chest, shoulders, triceps), Pull (back, biceps), and Legs. Ideal for muscle building and strength.

  • 📅 Frequency: 6 days/week
  • ⏱️ Duration: 60-90 mins per session
  • 🎯 Best for: Muscle growth & strength
Key Exercises:

Bench Press, Deadlifts, Squats, Barbell Rows, Dips, Pull-ups, Overhead Press, Leg Press, Barbell Curls, Tricep Rope Extensions
