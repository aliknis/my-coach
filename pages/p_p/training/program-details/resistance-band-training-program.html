Resistance Band Training

Portable resistance band workouts that train full-body strength with minimal equipment and joint-friendly loading.

Program Overview

  • Best for: Home users wanting scalable resistance training.
  • Frequency: 4 days/week
  • Session duration: 40-50 minutes
  • Main focus: Strength, tone, and muscular endurance

Suggested Weekly Structure

  • Day 1: Upper Push/Pull
  • Day 2: Lower Body
  • Day 3: Rest
  • Day 4: Full Body Bands
  • Day 5: Core + Mobility

Recommended Core Exercises

  • Band Chest Press
  • Band Row
  • Banded Squat
  • Band Overhead Press
  • Band Face Pull
  • Band Curl

Coaching Tips

  • Anchor bands safely before every set.
  • Increase tension gradually.
  • Pause at peak contraction for better activation.
