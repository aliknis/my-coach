Dumbbell Home Workout

Comprehensive dumbbell-only program for full-body strength and muscle gain from home.

Program Overview

  • Best for: Home trainees with adjustable dumbbells.
  • Frequency: 4-5 days/week
  • Session duration: 45-60 minutes
  • Main focus: Progressive strength and lean muscle

Suggested Weekly Structure

  • Day 1: Upper Push
  • Day 2: Lower Body
  • Day 3: Upper Pull
  • Day 4: Rest or mobility
  • Day 5: Full Body

Recommended Core Exercises

  • Dumbbell Bench Press
  • Goblet Squat
  • Dumbbell Row
  • Romanian Deadlift
  • Arnold Press
  • Renegade Row

Coaching Tips

  • Use unilateral work to fix imbalances.
  • Progress reps before increasing weight.
  • Maintain strict lifting form.
