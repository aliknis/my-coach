Yoga & Flexibility

Mobility and recovery focused flow to improve flexibility, posture, breathing, and stress management.

Program Overview

  • Best for: Recovery days, flexibility goals, and stress reduction.
  • Frequency: 3-5 days/week
  • Session duration: 30-45 minutes
  • Main focus: Flexibility, balance, and active recovery

Suggested Weekly Structure

  • Breathing and warm-up
  • Standing flow sequence
  • Hip opening sequence
  • Spinal mobility sequence
  • Relaxation and breath-down

Recommended Core Exercises

  • Downward Dog
  • Warrior Pose
  • Pigeon Pose
  • Tree Pose
  • Cobra Pose
  • Bridge Pose

Coaching Tips

  • Move with controlled breathing.
  • Never force painful ranges.
  • Practice consistently for long-term mobility gains.
