Upper/Lower Split

Alternating upper and lower sessions gives each muscle group two quality exposures per week for strength and size.

Program Overview

  • Best for: Lifters wanting a balanced strength/hypertrophy approach.
  • Frequency: 4 days/week
  • Session duration: 60-90 minutes
  • Main focus: Strength, power, and consistent progression

Suggested Weekly Structure

  • Day 1: Upper
  • Day 2: Lower
  • Day 3: Rest
  • Day 4: Upper
  • Day 5: Lower

Recommended Core Exercises

  • Bench Press
  • Barbell Row
  • Back Squat
  • Deadlift
  • Overhead Press
  • Pull-up

Coaching Tips

  • Keep main lifts in lower rep ranges.
  • Add accessory volume based on recovery.
  • Track top sets and back-off sets.
