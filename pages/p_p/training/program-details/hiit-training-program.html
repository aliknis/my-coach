HIIT Training

Short high-intensity intervals alternating effort and recovery to improve fitness and burn calories efficiently.

Program Overview

  • Best for: People with limited time and fat-loss goals.
  • Frequency: 3-4 days/week
  • Session duration: 20-30 minutes
  • Main focus: Cardio fitness, fat loss, and stamina

Suggested Weekly Structure

  • Warm-up 5 minutes
  • 6-10 interval rounds
  • Work 20-40 seconds
  • Rest 20-40 seconds
  • Cool-down 5 minutes

Recommended Core Exercises

  • Burpee
  • Jump Squat
  • High Knees
  • Mountain Climber
  • Speed Skater
  • Jump Lunge

Coaching Tips

  • Keep intensity high on work intervals.
  • Do not skip warm-up and cool-down.
  • Limit sessions to avoid over-fatigue.
