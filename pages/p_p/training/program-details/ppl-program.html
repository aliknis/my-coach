Push/Pull/Legs (PPL)

A three-day split that separates pushing, pulling, and leg movements for balanced strength and hypertrophy progress.

Program Overview

  • Best for: Intermediate to advanced lifters who can train frequently.
  • Frequency: 6 days/week
  • Session duration: 60-90 minutes
  • Main focus: Muscle growth and strength development

Suggested Weekly Structure

  • Day 1: Push
  • Day 2: Pull
  • Day 3: Legs
  • Day 4-6: Repeat cycle
  • Day 7: Rest and recovery

Recommended Core Exercises

  • Bench Press
  • Deadlift
  • Back Squat
  • Overhead Press
  • Barbell Row
  • Pull-up

Coaching Tips

  • Track progressive overload weekly.
  • Keep one complete rest day.
  • Deload every 6-8 weeks if fatigue builds.
